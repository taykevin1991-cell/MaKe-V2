About MaKe

The agency that
curates destinations

MaKe is a retail agency and commercial leasing consultant based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We believe every space has a story, and every brand deserves to tell it in the right location.

We help F&B outlets, wellness studios, fitness concepts, and lifestyle brands find and secure the right shoplot, retail lot, or commercial unit for their next outlet or expansion. On the developer side, we help fill retail floors with tenants that turn properties into destinations.

Whether you are opening your first shop or expanding to a second location in KL or PJ, we handle the scouting, negotiation, and placement so you can focus on your brand. Currently operating across Kuala Lumpur and Petaling Jaya.
