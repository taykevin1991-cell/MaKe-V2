About MaKe

The agency that
curates destinations

MaKe is a boutique retail consultancy and placemaking agency based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We believe every space has a story, and every brand deserves to tell it in the right location.

We work with brands across F&B, wellness, fitness, and lifestyle to find and secure spaces that truly fit. On the developer side, we help curate retail environments that draw the right tenants and turn properties into destinations.

Currently operating across Klang Valley, with expansion into Johor and Singapore underway.
